II · The Launch

Forbes Global Properties. South Africa.

Forbes Global Properties is a curated international network of independent luxury brokerages, partnered exclusively with Forbes Media.

We open the South African office to the media on 1 July 2026, with a coordinated local and international rollout. Through the network, member firms across more than 35 countries are connected to qualified high-net-worth buyers and tenants worldwide.

A select group of exceptional homes will be considered for inclusion within the campaign and its supporting collateral — the editorial, digital and broadcast storytelling that introduces the South African chapter to that global audience.

III · The Feature

A featured home, among a select few.

If selected, we would produce a considered body of professional film, photography and editorial content of the home, created as launch campaign collateral rather than a traditional sales asset.

The content would carry across our marketing video, press release, social and digital announcements, and the editorial introduction of the brand to local and international media.

From there, the property would be positioned on ForbesGlobalProperties.com and across the network's global digital platforms — available to every member office, and presented to a qualified international audience for both sale and rental.

IV · The Approach

Listed with discretion.

Inclusion in the launch campaign, and within the Forbes Global Properties distribution network, requires the home to be formally listed for sale. We propose a Price on Application listing, structured to enable inclusion within the campaign rather than to drive an active sales push.

Rental remains the priority, and that is where our energy sits. Any sale enquiries are managed quietly, with expectations carefully shaped, and never at the expense of the rental conversation.

Across the Forbes Global Properties platform, the property is positioned for both sale and rental — available to qualified international buyers and tenants alike, presented with discretion and the content telling the story.
